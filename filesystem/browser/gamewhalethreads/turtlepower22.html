A Quest for Treasure Knight
If you are reading this, then you, like me, might be looking for an ORIGINAL UNALTERED UNCENSORED Treasure Knight experience. This post essentially documents my journey trying to find and play a pure copy of Treasure Knight for my Treasure-Like Retrospective series (coming soon!!). But be warned. The path will test your hyperweb skills in ways you might not expect. If you are still willing to try, you will need three mythical items hidden is distant corners of the hyperweb.
  • Emulaton (The Salvaged)
  • Nano-DOS-ware (The Micro Controller)
  • Treasure Knight Original, guarded by Doctor Popup (Doc Pop)
Follow these instructions very carefully, lest your computer become a paperweight.
